Welcome to National Housing Development & Urban Renewal Company Limited. Our company provides a range of homes under its housing options. Some of the areas where these homes are constructed are Paynter’s, Denefields, Friar Hill and Royal Gardens.

 

Process on how to register:

  •  Persons who are interested in registering under the National Housing Development Program, are required to visit their bank or any other bank to seek pre-approval to make sure you’re the best candidate. After being pre-approved by the bank you will be giving a letter that needs to be brought to National Housing Development. Once National Housing Development receives the pre-approval letter, you will be required to complete an application form. Once the form is completed your information will be carefully review and entered into our database.

  •  National Housing Development wants everyone to be able to own a piece of this beautiful paradise. For persons who do not get pre-approval, we can arrange a meeting for you to meet with Mr. Warner at State Insurance Corporation. He will able to assist you to reach the requirement so that you too can become a home owner.
